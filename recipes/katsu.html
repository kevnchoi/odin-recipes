Chicken Katsu

Description

This is a recipe for katsu, which is one of my favorite foods.

I highly suggest everyone to try making this recipe at home!

Cook Time: 45 minutes

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless chicken breast
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup panko
  • 1 cup oil for frying
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Season the chicken breasts on both sides with salt and pepper
  2. Place the flour, egg, and panko crumbs into separate shallow dishes
  3. Coat the chicken breasts in flour, shaking off any excess
  4. Dip them into the egg, then press into the panko crumbs until well coated on both sides
  5. Heat 1/4 inch of oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat
  6. Place chicken in the hot oil and cook 3-4 minutes per side
  7. ENJOY!
